Hello everyone!
I recently attended the Vket Real 2024 Winter event and it was an absolute blast!
From the incredible booths to the amazing community interactions, everything was just perfect.

I got to explore a plethora of booths.
Each booth was a unique experience, Showcasing everything from realistic reproductions of common VRChat objects to booths where you could create an avatar on the spot or 3D scan yourself into a digital avatar.
I was particularly impressed by the booth from HIKKY Company.
There was a booth to create a VRM that could be completed on the web.
Don't be suprised yet.
VRChat users will be pleased. The file size of the created VRM is quite small, "Medium".
This is the rank that VRChat users struggle to achieve. (Obviously, as with other avatar creation software, there are also items to set fine details.)
To summarize, this service is quite commendable in that it can be created on the web,
whitch saves time and effort, allows even new users to quickly create their own personalities,
and file size of VRM is small.

you wanna use it? --> Avatar Maker

Overall, Vket Real was an unforgettable experience. If you ever get the chance to attend, I highly recommend it. It's a great way to immerse yourself in the latest VR innovations and meet some amazing people.

Looking forward to the next event!
